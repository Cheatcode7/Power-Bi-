## Problem Statement

Our case study is based on a computer hardware business which is facing challenges in dynamically changing market. Sales director decides to invest in data analysis project and he would like to build power BI dashboard that can give him real time sales insights. 

This Sales Analysis Dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of sales performance across key dimensions such as time period, product category, region, and customer segment. The dashboard is designed to help stakeholders identify trends, monitor KPIs, and make data-driven decisions. It includes metrics such as total revenue, units sold, average order value, top-performing products, and sales growth rates. Interactive filters and visualizations enable deep dives into specific time frames, regions, or product lines to uncover actionable insights and optimize sales strategy.
